Music Love God

Mohan Nataraj
May 8, 2019 2 min read Music, Love, God

It’s quite unnerving sometime on what you stumble upon on the internet. In today’s world of music where lyrics mean nothing some just keep it alive. This is a post on what I just stumbled upon - this beautiful song, if you ever struggled or ever been through your downside of life or you are now feeling low, this a beautiful song that can touch your soul. Yep, music touches your soul like love does. Sometimes when I’m sad or depressed I just play loud music with my headphone (noise cancelling 😄) on and sleep. This is a great way or only way that helps me get over alot of sadness in life. Grateful for alot of things that I have in my life while these beautiful emotions that touches your soul - sadness, difficulty, music and love has only made me a better and stronger person today.


Isn’t it true : “God only knows what you’ve been through
God only knows what they say about you
God only knows how it’s killing you
But there’s a kind of love that God only knows
God only knows the real you”

Hope this song helps you, makes your day better and put a damn smile on that face. #Loveyouself #StayStrong #GodOnlyKnows

Oh!! by the way if you need hope & you just haven’t seen it yet, here you go… this is a beautiful song too…

Once your done listening to knowing GodOnlyKnows and haven’t seen it yet you should :

“Burn the ships, cut the ties
Send a flare into the night
Say a prayer, turn the tide
Dry your tears and wave goodbye
Step into a new day
We can rise up from the dust and walk away
We can dance upon our heartache, yeah
So light a match, leave the past, burn the ships
And don’t you look back”


If you ever reached here and reading this, feel free to share a song or songs that touched your soul in the comment below.
