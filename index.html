Graduate Research Assistant

Illinois Institute of Technology

Mathematical Economics - In this research project we worked on developing a combinatorial algorithm to compute market equilibrium for Arrow Debreu – exchange economic market model with a special case of utility function called - Leontif utility function. This is an open problem and is a part of computational complexity class called PPAD. As a part of research work I reviewed the literature and designed a mathematical model using the techniques of convex programming. We applied concepts of auction theory – bidding, graph theory and multiplicative weight update algorithm as a set of rules to be followed by the program. While this research still requires further improvement it has its application in online ad word games.

Sentimental Analysis of Tweets - This research project was in conjunction with the students from Department of Psychology - here the students identified certain tweets and we developed an interface which would collect the tweets using api’s .
